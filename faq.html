General

Technical

Extract the downloaded .rar file, copy the folder to your Minecraft mods folder. The launcher automatically handles the required settings.

First, check your internet connection. Then ask for help in the #support channel on our Discord server. Our team is ready 24/7.

Lower your render distance in Minecraft settings, set graphics quality to "Fast". We also recommend using Optifine.

Gameplay

PvP is optional. You can enable/disable it with the /pvp command. The spawn area is always safe.

Use the /claim command to claim the land you're standing on. New players start with 5000 claim blocks and earn 100 more blocks for every hour of play.

Seasonal resets occur approximately every 6-8 months and are determined by community vote.
